Overview

Operation Market Garden was an ambitious Allied military operation during World War II, aimed at securing key bridges in the Netherlands to allow a swift advance into Germany.

Strategic Goals

The operation was divided into two parts: 'Market,' the airborne assault to capture the bridges, and 'Garden,' the ground offensive to link up with the paratroopers.
